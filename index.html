Campus Life

School Library

Was established in 2005

The Marriotts Ridge High School Library contains hundreds of books which any student can borrow for free. There are many different genres of books, including fiction, non-fiction, and comics. There are also many computers for students to use in the library and there is a printer which students and teachers can use.



School Auditorium

Was established in 2005

The Marriotts Ridge High School Auditorium contains hundreds of seats designed to hold a big audience for the concerts held in the auditorium. There is also a big stage for the concert members to perform. There are microphones on the stage and speakers around the audtiroum, making it easier for the audience to hear the performances held in here.



School Cafeteria

Was established in 2005

The Marriotts Ridge High School Cafeteria contains 15 tables and benches for students to eat their lunch. It also contains a kitchen where the lunch is made for students who want to buy lunch at school. There are microwaves around the cafeteria for students to use. Students may also eat their lunch on the benches outside, but it may sometimes be too cold.
